Features

Many Referral Programs

Two options to build a strong, effective referral/reward campaign. Either create stand-alone referral/reward program using open-source APIs, or participate in Plentix’s referral ecosystem and share a common digital currency.

Innovative architecture

Plentix seamlessly plugs into companies retailing sale or POS systems, but users see one interface and only sign up once to Plentix application.

Flexible reward options

Referrers may get a reward in tokens for every active referral, or even get commission for first three purchases; it is up to the business to choose.

Decentralized and automated

Smart contracts automation - allows programmers to easily plug and play with supported software, enabling reduced cost with effortless scaling capabilities and automation.

Access To Tokenized Referral

Every time businesses and customers connect, every recommendation for a friend, the Plentix token stores and transfers value. Plentix rewards referrers, referees, businesses and developers (#4WIN)

Advanced Features

Using Plasma’s solution which makes transactions on side chain, Plentix has higher speed and much lower cost of transactions compared to its competitors, circumnavigating Ethereum’s scalability problems.
